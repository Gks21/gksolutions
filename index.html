Services

How I can help

Each service starts with a question: what's getting in your way? These are the areas where I work most often, always tailored to your goals.

Custom software & apps

When off-the-shelf tools don't fit how you work.

If spreadsheets, paper forms, or workarounds are slowing your team down, custom software can fit your process instead of forcing you to change.

  • Tracking built around how you actually manage customers and orders
  • Custom tools designed around your team's daily workflow
  • Systems your people can reach from anywhere, on any device

Workflow automation

When the same tasks eat hours every week.

Copying data between programs, running reports, sending the same updates, repetitive work adds up. Automation connects your tools so that work happens reliably in the background.

  • Programs that talk to each other without manual copying
  • Reports that arrive when you need them, not when someone remembers
  • Everyday processes that run the same way every time

IT consulting

When you need a clear plan before making changes.

Not every problem needs a new app. Sometimes you need honest guidance; What to upgrade, what to keep, and how to stay secure without overspending.

  • Technology decisions you can understand before you commit
  • Stronger security and reliability without unnecessary complexity
  • Modernizing older systems at a pace that works for you

Web design & development

When your online presence doesn't reflect you.

Your website is often the first impression people get. It should look professional, work on any device, and be something you can update without calling for help every time.

  • Sites that help visitors find what they need quickly
  • Redesigns that fix what's confusing or outdated
  • Hosting and maintenance guidance you can count on
